---

Run the Cohort Analysis exercise from `~/.claude/skills/retention/SKILL.md` (Stage 3: Cohort Analysis).

1. Ask what metric they're tracking (users, revenue, activity)
2. Ask for their cohort data (monthly or weekly signups and retention numbers)
3. Build the cohort retention table with percentage retention per period
4. Identify the pattern: The Cliff, The Plateau, The Smile, or The Slide
5. Diagnose root cause based on the pattern
6. Compare newer vs older cohorts — is the product improving?
7. Identify the critical drop-off point (which month/week has the biggest loss)
8. Recommend specific actions tied to the pattern

If the curve never flattens, that's a PMF problem, not a retention problem. Be honest about it.
