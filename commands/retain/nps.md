Run the NPS/CSAT exercise from `~/.claude/skills/retention/SKILL.md` (Stage 2: NPS/CSAT Surveys).

1. Ask whether they need NPS (overall relationship) or CSAT (specific touchpoint)
2. Ask about their product and user base (determines segmentation strategy)
3. Design the survey: question wording, scale, follow-up question
4. Define sending triggers and timing (when to send, who to send to)
5. Set up the scoring framework (NPS: promoters/passives/detractors; CSAT: satisfaction bands)
6. Design the segmentation plan (by plan tier, usage level, tenure, industry)
7. Define actions for each score band — what do you DO with a detractor vs promoter?
8. Provide benchmarks for their industry

The overall score is less useful than the segment analysis. Always segment.
