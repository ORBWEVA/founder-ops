Run the Offer Letter exercise from `~/.claude/skills/hiring/SKILL.md` (Stage 4: Offer Letter).

1. Ask about the role (title, level, reporting to)
2. Ask about compensation (base salary, equity, bonus, signing bonus)
3. Ask about company stage (seed, Series A — determines equity benchmarks)
4. Generate the full offer letter template with all sections
5. Include the equity benchmarks table for their stage and role
6. Explain ISO vs NSO implications
7. Flag "TALK TO A LAWYER" items (equity terms, 409A valuation, tax implications)
8. Include conditions (background check, IP assignment, confidentiality)
9. Set an expiration date (5-7 business days)

Close fast. Good candidates have options. Extend offers within 48 hours of final interview.
