Run the ToS exercise from `~/.claude/skills/legal-essentials/SKILL.md` (Stage 1: Terms of Service).

1. Ask what type of product (SaaS, marketplace, API, mobile app, e-commerce)
2. Ask what jurisdictions they serve (US, EU, UK, AU, global)
3. Ask if they have payment/billing (free trial, subscriptions, one-time)
4. Ask if users create content (UGC implications)
5. Generate the full ToS template customized to their answers
6. Flag every "TALK TO A LAWYER" item and explain WHY it needs review
7. List jurisdiction-specific requirements they must address

Remind them: this is a template, not legal advice. A lawyer should review before publishing.
