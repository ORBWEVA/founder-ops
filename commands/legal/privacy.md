Run the Privacy Policy exercise from `~/.claude/skills/legal-essentials/SKILL.md` (Stage 2: Privacy Policy).

1. Ask what data they collect (personal info, payment, usage, cookies, UGC)
2. Ask what jurisdictions their users are in (determines GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, APPs)
3. Ask about third-party services (analytics, payment processors, email tools)
4. Ask if they collect any sensitive data (health, financial, children's)
5. Generate the full privacy policy template with all applicable sections
6. Include jurisdiction-specific rights sections (GDPR rights, CCPA rights)
7. Flag every "TALK TO A LAWYER" item, especially around international data transfers
8. List sub-processors they need to document

Remind them: this is a template, not legal advice. A lawyer should review before publishing.
