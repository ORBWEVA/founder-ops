---

Run the Contracts exercise from `~/.claude/skills/legal-essentials/SKILL.md` (Stage 3: Contracts).

1. Ask which contract type they need (contractor, advisor, NDA)
2. For contractor agreements:
   a. Ask about scope of work and compensation structure
   b. Run the contractor vs employee classification checklist
   c. Flag reclassification risk if 3+ employee signals
3. For advisor agreements:
   a. Ask about role type (strategic, technical, industry)
   b. Ask about time commitment and term
   c. Provide equity benchmarks for their stage
4. For NDAs:
   a. Ask if mutual or one-way
   b. Ask about term and scope
   c. Warn them: VCs don't sign NDAs
5. Generate the full contract template with all sections
6. Flag every "TALK TO A LAWYER" item

Remind them: these are templates, not legal advice. A lawyer should review before signing.
